What is STEM

STEM - standing for Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics - is a relatively new strand of learning within the education system, and its aim is to explore and incorporate these subjects into the curriculum, as it provides students with problem solving and critical thinking skills in ways that other subjects do not. With recent developments in technology, the education sector has not caught up with recent advancements, so our aim is to aid the STEM movement by teaching everyone about it.

Why bother with STEM? -

With STEM being a new facet of the education system, but with its exponential growth within the world's job oppotunity and innovations stage, we discovered many thousands of educational institutions still have not made the change to cope with this ever evolving technology.

More about us...

We all have backgrounds in STEM, and had excellent mentors growing up, and were involved in a program called FIRST robotics, which intruduced us to STEM in one of the best ways.

So, who are we? -

We are a bunch of creators, that want to see the best come of our next generation of people, and we belive in education being the means of creative thinking, and new ideas. With these new ideas, the world can be in better hands in the future.

We believe that the best way to inform is to educate, but in the most exciting ways possible, which means truly getting students to understand concepts through first hand experience. We say although learning theory is good, to really engage and play around in subjects, students tend to engage more with practical learning.
