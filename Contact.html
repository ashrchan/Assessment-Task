Please go head on over to these sites, who have genuinely good STEM programs, which have inspired the creation of this company! Their efforts have inspired thousands globally.

FIRST Robotics Competitions

FIRST, which stands for For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology, founded by Dean Camen, is a series of global competitions which involves teams creating robots to compete against each other in an annually held game/challenge. For meeting other people inspired by STEM, this is one of the best ways to see STEM in action.

Some more links...

YMCA Space Squad

Located in Canberra, YMCA runs an amazing space squad for students in grades 6-10. It holds a week long program, where students are thrown into group activities, and experience space and science in places all around canberra. One of the best space programs in Australian area, you should check it out!

CASE Space Squad

Although more expensive than the other option above on this page, if you're dedicated to learning about Space and Engineering, this is definitely a program for you! More focused in the Space side of STEM, this program gets students to visit America, and tour NASA's sites.
