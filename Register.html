Registering -

Whatever company registers with us, we aim to give your teachers and students the best opportunities, and to do this, we tailor our courses to your company. This means that you need to do the following steps -

Step One

Contact us! we would love to hear from you, and you can find us through email, Instagram, Facebook and LinkedIn. Just search for Project Inspire and you should find us. Alternatively, check out our Contact Us page, which has our links.

If we don't contact you in the proceeding 48 hours, feel free to re-email, or re-send any information forward.

Step Two

Once we are in contact, our teams need to figure out the best programs for you, and what your company wants to get out of the different STEM programs. We will hopefully set up a call, and Project Inspire will be in contact with some of your teachers/mentors in hopes that there is a program that fits your want.

Step Three

Project Inspire comes to you!

We go ahead and engage your teachers and students in our programs, and we hope that from this experience, we can gain feedback and hopefully improve our programs for the future!
